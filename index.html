ACE Style Survey

Welcome!

The purpose of this site is to evaluate different designs and layouts for the upcoming Academic Course Evaluation application. The intent is to make the evaluations easy to understand and fill out. You will be reviewing 2 different workflows for course evalautions.

While reviewing the pages, please keep in mind that you may be doing an evaluation for each of your courses. What would encourage you to fill out an evaluation? What would prevent you from filling out an evaluation? What was easy? What wasn't?

Instructions
